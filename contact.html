Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the warranty?

All our products come with a 3-year comprehensive warranty covering manufacturing defects and performance issues.

What is the delivery time?

We deliver within 5-7 business days to most locations. Express delivery options are available for select cities.

Do you offer EMI options?

Yes! We offer flexible EMI options with leading banks and financial institutions. No interest EMI for selected products.

How do I maintain my e-scooter?

Regular maintenance includes cleaning, checking tire pressure, and periodic battery checks. Visit our service centers for professional care.

What is the battery lifespan?

Our lithium-ion batteries are designed to last 5+ years with proper maintenance. Battery health can be monitored via our mobile app.

Do you have test ride facilities?

Yes! You can book a test ride at any of our authorized dealers. Check our website for the nearest location.
