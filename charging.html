Charging Tips & FAQ

What's the fastest way to charge?

DC Fast Charging is the quickest option, reaching 80% in 20-30 minutes. For daily charging, Level 2 is more economical and gentle on your battery.

Can I charge at home?

Absolutely! We provide home charging installation services starting at $2,500. It's the most convenient and cost-effective way to charge daily.

How much does charging cost?

Costs vary by location and charger type. Level 2 typically costs $0.15-$0.25/kWh, while DC fast charging is $0.30-$0.40/kWh. Check real-time pricing in the app.

Is the network safe?

Yes! All stations are regularly maintained, monitored 24/7, and equipped with safety features. We maintain a 99.9% uptime guarantee.

Can I charge different EV brands?

Our stations are compatible with all EVs using standard connectors (CCS, CHAdeMO, Tesla, etc.). Stackly vehicles have exclusive priority access.

What happens if a charger is broken?

If you encounter a non-working charger, report it in the app immediately. We'll refund your transaction and provide directions to the nearest alternative.
